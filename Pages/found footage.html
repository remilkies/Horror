Found-Footage Movies

Found footage films are my personal favorite form of story telling. Instead of a high budget heavily edited and put together feature film, you get grimy first hand look directly through the eyes of the characters or victims in the films. Get immursed in the film and believe that what your watching is real found footage, never before seen by anyone except those that might have died capturing it
