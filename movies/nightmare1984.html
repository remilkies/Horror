A Nightmare on Elm Street

When someone is chasing you, has broken into your house or trying to kill you, you have some options: call the police, scream until someone hears you or fight (possibly to the death). When someone is chasing you in your dreams, in a nightmare, we all understand the feeling of hopelessness. Not a person or phone in sight, all you can do is run until you wake up...if you do.
